Lasagna

How to make lasagna

When John Chandler submitted this lasagna recipe to Allrecipes.com more than 20 years ago, he had no idea how successful it would become. One of the top-performing recipes of all time, World's Best Lasagna racks up more than 7 million views per year and has ranked among the most popular lasagna recipes on the internet for two decades. Unfortunately, John unexpectedly passed away at 53 years old.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients

  • Meat:
    • Italian Sausage
    • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato Products:
    • Can of crushed tomatoes
    • Two cans of tomato sauce
    • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices & seasonings:
    • Fresh parsley
    • Dried basil leaves
    • Salt
    • Italian seasoning
    • Fennel seeds
    • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses:
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
